Education

Welcome to my website. I stripped it down to just HTML and new.css after becoming dissatisfied with the amount of bloat on the modern web.

I'm interested in the intersection of technology, business, and public policy. My main goal is to create a world that is more free and just than the one I inherited.

Currently, I'm based in Toronto, Ontario as a Business Systems Analyst at CIBC, one of Canada's big 5 banks. There, I work on the next generation of finance and HR applications powering the future of banking.

I'm studying business and computer science at the University of British Columbia Sauder School of Business. I also sit on the Commerce Undergraduate Society's Board of Directors, governing the strategy of Western Canada's largest business students' union.

In my free time, I enjoy practicing the many instruments I've picked up over the years (piano, saxophone, guitar, ukelele), running long distances, and practicing my French - I'm aiming to get past the A2 level of proficiency by the end of the year.
