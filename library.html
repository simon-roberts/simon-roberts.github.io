Library

Here's a list of books that I've found interesting or insightful. However, an complete endorsement of the contents or theses is not a prerequisite for a title's inclusion. Several of the works have competiting arguments.

I prefer reading these in print, but I've read some on my computer and listened to a few more. I'm not particularly concerned with the medium of communication, but rather I'm focused on developing a solid understanding of the author's intent.

If you have any thoughts you'd like to share with me on these titles, to reach out to me at simonneilroberts(at)gmail.com.

  • Newport, Cal - How to Become a Straight-A Student
  • Newport, Cal - How to Win at College
  • Sowell, Thomas - Basic Economics
  • Malkiel, Burton - A Random Walk Down Wall Street
  • Graham, Benjamin - The Intelligent Investor
  • Dalio, Ray - Principles
  • Housel, Morgan - The Psychology of Money
  • Kaczynski, Theodore - Technological Slavery
  • Orwell, George - Animal Farm
  • Orwell, George - 1984
